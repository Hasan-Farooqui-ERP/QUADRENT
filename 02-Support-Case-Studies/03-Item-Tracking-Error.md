# Case Study — Item Tracking Error on Assembly Order

## Summary
Assembly Orders failed due to missing serial numbers.

## Problem
Error: “Serial No. is required for Item XYZ-APPAREL.”

## Root Cause
Item Tracking Code misconfigured (Lot only).

## Resolution
1. Enabled Serial Tracking.
2. Updated Item Card.
3. Revalidated Assembly Order.

## Prevention
- Item Tracking Checklist

## Evidence
Screenshots: Item Tracking Code, Assembly Order.
